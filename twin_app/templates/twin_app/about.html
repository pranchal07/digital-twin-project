About Digital Twin Student Health System

Welcome to the Digital Twin Student Health System — a comprehensive web application designed to help students monitor their physical health, lifestyle habits, and academic performance.

This system integrates advanced AI-powered analytics and personalized health risk predictions to empower users in making informed decisions about their well-being.

Features

  • Monitor essential vitals, lifestyle, and academic data in one platform.
  • Get personalized, actionable recommendations through AI.
  • Track goals and progress with intuitive dashboards and charts.
  • Privacy-first design — all your data stays secure and under your control.

Technology Stack

Built with Django for a robust backend, powered by scikit-learn for machine learning models, and a modern, responsive frontend with Chart.js and CSS custom themes.
